Weightlifting is a sport without mercy, it is very tough to make progress. It is not really fun like football where you are part of a team. 22 guys are chasing a ball and you don’t even notice the effort you are putting in. In weightlifting on the other hand, every time you touch the bar you realise that you are always alone in weightlifting, the weights are getting heavier and heavier, everything hurts, it is simply a very long journey until it gets to be fun.
